Tsono — Pre-Seed Opportunity | DFW
Hi there,

I'm TK. — I'm building Tsono Moto App out of Dallas-Fort Worth, and I think you'll want to see this.

Tsono is the riding app motorcycle culture has been waiting for. Not a fitness tracker with a bike skin. A purpose-built platform for how riders actually ride, connect, and spend.

Why DFW. Why now.

Texas has more registered motorcycles than almost any state, and DFW is the 4th largest metro in the country — 8 million people, year-round riding weather, and a motorcycle culture that runs deep. There's no dominant digital platform serving these riders. We're changing that, starting here.

What's live today:

• Route planning & navigation with community-shared rides
• Garage management — bikes, maintenance, mods
• Group rides with real-time location sharing
• Social feeds, messaging, and local event calendars
• Marketplace foundations for gear, parts, and bikes

The platform is a PWA built on Web Technologies — no app store fees, instant updates, works on every device. Fully functional demo ready at 'lets have a chat'.

Why riders need Tsono:
800K+ registered motorcycles in Texas
70% of riders prefer group rides

Sources: Texas DMV, Motorcycle Industry Council
